🎯 May Move! Bingo — Full Rules

Complete a full row every week. Complete all 4 weeks to enter the smoothie draw.

How It Works

May is a 4 week challenge. The same Bingo card runs every week. Complete a full row — across, down or diagonal — each week. Collect all 4 weekly completions and you will be entered into the lucky draw for a smoothie at the end of the month.

  • The card resets every Monday. A new week = a fresh card.
  • Each completed bingo card will earn you a lucky draw entry.
  • You do not carry squares over from one week to the next.
  • You can play as an individual or as a team of up to 3 people. A team shares one card.
  • A team needs to select a captain to record their results.
  • If you play as a team, each square only needs to be completed by one team member. Your choice how you split it — that's between you and your conscience.

Combining Activities

You do not have to do one single activity to qualify for a Moderate or Challenge square. You can combine two activities in the same week to reach the next level.

✅ Two Light activities in a week = qualifies for one Moderate square.

✅ Two Moderate activities in a week = qualifies for one Challenge square.

Mix and match freely — a Light swim and a Light gym session counts as one Moderate square. A Moderate run and a Moderate cycle counts as one Challenge square. The combination must happen within the same weekly card period.

Special Squares

WILDCARD

  • Nominate your own intentional, recorded activity on WhatsApp before you do it.
  • It must be something you will actively track — Strava, fitness watch, or equivalent.
  • It cannot duplicate a square already on the card.
  • Once completed, post your record to the group to confirm.
  • Incidental movement does not count — the activity must be deliberate and logged.

FREE

  • Post a team photo of your group doing something active in the WhatsApp group.
  • The Free square resets every week — you need a new photo each week to claim it.
  • One photo counts for the whole team.
  • The activity must be real and intentional — not a staged photo of people standing still.

ELITE

  • The Elite square counts as one tick — one activity, one square, regardless of how far above the threshold you go.
  • It does not auto-claim any other square.
  • Examples: a half marathon (21km+), a 50km+ cycle, a 3km+ swim, or 5 gym sessions in one week.

RECOVERY

  • Specifically included for Comrades Marathon runners tapering in May, and for anyone who needs a lighter week.
  • Foam rolling, mobility work, gentle yoga, or a rest-day stretch all qualify.
  • No minimum duration — do what your body needs.
  • Must still be intentional and logged.

Weekly Structure

May has four weekly card periods. The first and last weeks include a few extra days to cover the full month.

Week 11–7 May
Week 28–14 May
Week 315–21 May
Week 422–31 May (extended)

Complete a full row in each weekly period. All 4 completions enter you into the monthly draw. Partial completions do not count toward the draw but are still celebrated on the WhatsApp group.

🥤 The Smoothie Draw

  • Everyone who completes all 4 weekly cards goes into the draw.
  • The draw takes place at the start of June.
  • Winners receive a smoothie — because you earned it.
  • Teams enter the draw as one entry — winning the draw wins the smoothie for the whole team.

Tracking & Recording

  • Log your activities on Strava or post to the WhatsApp group as your record.
  • Each square must be claimed with evidence — a Strava activity, a screenshot, or a WhatsApp post.
  • No evidence, no tick.
  • Wildcard squares must be nominated before the activity — post your intention first, then your completion.
  • When you complete a row, post your completed card to the WhatsApp group to claim your weekly completion.
"This is not about being the fittest person here. It is about showing up every week, moving your body, and building a habit that lasts beyond May. Whether you run a half marathon or take your first 20-minute walk, every square you tick is a genuine win. Train smart. Rest when you need to. Move together."
